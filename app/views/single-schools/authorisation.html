Agree to the Energy for Schools letter of authority

This letter of authority sets out the arrangements agreed between the Department for Education ('DfE')and [school/trust name] (the 'Education Provider') in connection with the provision of energy supplies.

This letter supersedes and replaces all previous agreements between DfE and the Education Provider in relation to the supply of gas and/or electricity.

DfE has entered into and/or will enter into one or more Framework Agreements with various suppliers for the provision of gas and/or electricity to DfE and to various sites identified by DfE. Each such energy supplier is referred to in this letter as a 'Supplier'. Under the Framework Agreements, DfE is entitled to request a Supplier to provide gas and/or electricity to locations identified by DfE.

The Education Provider has requested and DfE has agreed to arrange for the supply of gas and/or electricity to the following Site(s):

  • [school/trust name], [school address]

Appointment

The Education Provider appoints and authorises DfE to act on behalf of the Education Provider to negotiate and conclude arrangements with the relevant Supplier for the supply of gas and/or electricity to theSite(s).

The Education Provider acknowledges that DfE's appointment includes:

  • obtaining consumption and billing data for the purposes of conducting consumption and cost analysis
  • obtaining contractual details (contract durations and terms) for existing energy supplies
  • notifying energy suppliers of or initiating 'Change of Tenancies' (COT's) or Novations
  • notifying energy suppliers of or initiating 'Change of Address' for any of the School/Academy's accounts or billing addresses
  • providing annual trading reports and monthly consolidated data reports to help budget forecasting
  • taking responsibility for the contractual arrangements between DfE and the Suppliers

but does not include:

  • terminating existing supply contracts
  • obtaining supply price quotations from energy suppliers for consideration or acceptance by the Education Provider
  • claiming or obtaining refunds from Suppliers on billing 'over-charges' following analysis
  • undertaking any public procurement activities or notices

Responsibilities

The Education Provider acknowledges and agrees that it is responsible for:

  • paying the charges (and any interest) for the gas and/or electricity supplied (as set out above)
  • notifying the Supplier as soon as reasonably practicable if it believes that there has been any damage or change to, or interference with any metering equipment used for the supply of energy and/or recording of energy consumption
  • protecting the metering equipment against weather damage, physical damage and any unauthorised third party interference or adjustment
  • where required, providing meter readings to the Supplier for the purposes of calculating energy consumption and the associated charges
  • providing safe and reasonable access to the Supplier, its employees and agents for purposes related to the supply of gas and/or electricity including inspecting, testing, maintaining, repairing and replacing any metering equipment

Invoices and billing

The Education Provider acknowledges and agrees that DfE shall direct the relevant Supplier to issue invoices directly to the Education Provider in relation to the gas and/or electricity consumed at the Site(s).

The Education Provider agrees that it will be responsible for and shall pay the charges set out in the relevant invoices in accordance with the payment terms agreed between the relevant energy supplier and the Education Provider. In the case of any late payments, the Education Provider shall be liable for interest in accordance with the payment terms advised by the relevant Supplier.

If, because of the Education Provider not paying any invoiced charges on time or at all in accordance with this letter, DfE is required to make payment of any sums to the Supplier, then DfE shall be entitled to deduct and retain an equivalent sum of money from any funding payable by DfE to the Education Provider under the relevant DfE Conditions of Funding/Funding Agreement. This right is without prejudice to and in addition to any other rights of DfE to withhold or suspend payment under the relevant conditions of funding, 0funding agreement or funding rules.
