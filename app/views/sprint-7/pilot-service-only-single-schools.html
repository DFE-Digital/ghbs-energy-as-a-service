Service only available for single schools

The online service does not currently allow for multiple schools to join Energy for Schools using a single form.

If your school belongs to a multi-academy trust (MAT), you can either:

  • use the online service for each individual school within the MAT

    • (or)

  • contact dfe-energy.services-team@education.gov.uk to join using paper forms

The online service is due to open to MATs on 7 July 2025.
